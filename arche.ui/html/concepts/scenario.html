Scenario

A quality attribute scenario (or simply 'scenario') expresses a specific non-functional requirement of the system. Contains up to six parts:
  • Stimulus
  • Source of stimulus
  • Environment
  • Artifact being stimulated
  • Response
  • Response measure
Example: A garage door must detect an obstacle and halt within .1 seconds.

Scenarios are used to characterize desired quality attributes of a system. In ArchE, a scenario is associated with a specific quality. The information contained in the six parts of a scenario is essential for ArchE to create the final design.
